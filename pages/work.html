Case Study: Northwest Montessori 60th Anniversary Video Client: Northwest Montessori School (Seattle, WA) Project: 60th Anniversary Fundraiser Video (Short-Form + Future Long-Form Vision) Role: Creative Direction, Story Development, Editing, Production Strategy Team: Cascade Creatives (Jeremy & Melissa) ⸻ Overview Northwest Montessori School approached us to help create a meaningful video for their 60th anniversary fundraiser. The goal was to capture the heart of the school’s legacy—its people, philosophy, and sense of community—while also creating something concise and impactful for a live event setting. With limited time and a need to involve a wide range of voices from the school’s history, we designed a storytelling approach that balanced emotional depth with production efficiency. ⸻ Approach Rather than relying solely on traditional filming, we developed a hybrid production strategy: • Guided current and former students, teachers, and administrators to record personal reflections on their phones • Conducted key interviews with leadership, including the current Head of School (Elizabeth) and former director (Jan) • Incorporated archival photos and school history to visually connect past and present • Built a narrative around the theme: “Rooted in Legacy, Growing Our Future” This approach allowed us to gather authentic, heartfelt content from across generations while staying within budget and timeline constraints. ⸻ Creative Direction We shaped the story around three core ideas: 1. Legacy & Origin • Highlighting founder Marietta and the symbolic “Expect a Miracle” cards 2. Community as Home • Emphasizing that NW Montessori is not just a school, but a place to come home to 3. Future Growth • Inspiring continued involvement and support for the next generation The 90-second film was intentionally crafted as a cinematic teaser—designed to resonate emotionally during the fundraiser while opening the door for a future, more expansive story. ⸻ Execution • Edited a 90-second narrative-driven video optimized for live event presentation • Selected and refined short, impactful soundbites from multiple contributors • Designed a visual style using: • Subtle photo animations (Ken Burns effect) • Clean transitions and pacing • Warm, cinematic tone • Structured the edit to build from nostalgia → community → inspiration ⸻ Results • Delivered a compelling, emotionally engaging piece for the school’s 60th anniversary fundraiser • Created a scalable storytelling foundation for: • A longer-form documentary version • Ongoing monthly community spotlight videos • Strengthened connection between past and present members of the NW Montessori community ⸻ Key Takeaway By combining thoughtful storytelling with a flexible production model, we helped Northwest Montessori tell a 60-year story in just 90 seconds—while setting the stage for deeper storytelling in the future.
