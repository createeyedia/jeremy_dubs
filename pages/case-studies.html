Website Upgrade — Bellevue, WA
All Star Lake Spa

A lakeside massage and medical spa serving the greater Seattle/Eastside area since 2006.

Homepage Rewrite Spam Removal Nav Cleanup Services Restructure CTA Improvements Mobile Layout Visual Redesign

The Situation

All Star Lake Spa has been a trusted name in the Bellevue wellness community for nearly two decades — offering medical massage, facials, body treatments, lash extensions, and spa packages. Their reputation is strong. Their website wasn't keeping pace.

The site had several compounding problems: a cluttered homepage that buried the most important information, a services section that was hard to navigate, a mobile layout that broke on smaller screens, and — most critically — injected spam content that had appeared on the services page, advertising vaping products. That content was actively undermining trust and potentially harming their search rankings.

What I Fixed

Removed injected spam content (vaping product promotion) from the services page
Rewrote homepage headline and intro copy to lead with clarity and trust
Restructured the services section — organized into logical categories, easier to scan
Improved booking CTAs — clearer language, better placement, more visible on mobile
Cleaned up and simplified the main navigation
Fixed mobile layout — improved tap targets, readable text sizes, consistent spacing
Visual redesign to better reflect the quality and calm of the actual spa experience
The Before — Key Issues Identified
Homepage
No clear headline above the fold
Multiple competing CTAs with no hierarchy
Generic welcome copy that told visitors nothing useful
Services Page
Injected vaping product spam visible to all visitors
Services listed as links only — no descriptions
No pricing context or way to compare options
Mobile
Layout broke on smaller screens
Phone number not tappable
Booking button hard to find

Ongoing Video Production — Western Washington
Cascade Pest Control

A regional pest control company serving homeowners and businesses across Western Washington.

Social Reels Commercials Seasonal Campaigns YouTube Content Ongoing Retainer Brand Video

The Partnership

Cascade Pest Control needed consistent, professional video content to stay visible across platforms throughout the year. Pest control is a highly seasonal business — ant season, wasp season, rodent season, holiday prep — which means there's always a relevant story to tell if the content is planned and produced well.

The challenge with most local service businesses is they either produce no video content at all, or they produce it inconsistently and it shows. Cascade Pest Control wanted to be different: a regular stream of professional content that keeps the brand top of mind and drives calls during peak seasons.

What I Produce

Social media reels and short-form clips for Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok
Seasonal campaign videos tied to pest activity cycles throughout the year
Commercial and brand videos for paid media and website use
YouTube content — longer-form educational and brand videos
Ongoing editorial calendar alignment so content is planned, not reactive
